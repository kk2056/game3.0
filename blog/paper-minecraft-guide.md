Gameplay Tips: Controlling the Ragdoll
You only have one button (Up Arrow or W). This button makes your players jump and flail their arms.
The Double Jump: You can often tap the button twice quickly. The first tap lifts you, and the second tap can rotate your player's body. Use this to angle your shot.
Defense is Offense: If you can't get the ball, jump into the opponent. Knocking them over is a valid strategy. If they fall, they can't shoot.
The Long Shot: Sometimes, holding the jump button slightly longer adds more power to the throw. Experiment with tap duration.
Adaptation Strategies
The "Random" in the title refers to changing conditions every round.
Heavy Ball: When the ball is heavy (iron), don't try long shots. Dribble (hop) closer to the hoop before shooting.
Small Hoops: Precision is key here. Wait for the opponent to miss, then take a calm shot.
Long Arms/Big Heads: These modes change the center of gravity. Adjust your jump timing accordingly.
